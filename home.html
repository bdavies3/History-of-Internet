Welcome!

This is the landing page for our collaborative project on the History of The Internet. From here, you can navigate to our About page to learn a little bit more about the project, and the Team page for more info on ourselves. Included below and on our navbar are pages that guide you through the history of this technology from its inception to the bleeding edge, step by step.

Table of Contents:

    Part I: The History of the Internet
    An overview of the technology and people behind what would become the Internet, beginning as a Cold War research experiment.
    Part II: The Browser Wars
    A look into the driving forces behind the software that powers our Internet today, and how the browser came to be.
    Part III: Internet Search
    The next piece of the puzzle, how to make finding what you're looking for on the Web palatable, and profitable.
    Part IV: Dot Com Bubble
    In which the winners and losers of a historic boom and bust are decided, and how it changed the Internet landscape as we know it
    Part V: People
    Now plentiful and in the hands of the people, The Internet is looked to as a beacon for progress, with its own set of challenges.
